Stubborn

I am a very stubborn person. For me, being stuubborn affects my home and school life by making me unable to give up on any thing. If I have an assignment or task to complete, I will absolutely do it because I refuse to fail at anything.

Helpful

I would consider myself to be a helpful person. I believe that you should treat others the way that you want to be treated. Hence, if there is anyone at home or school who needs help and I can provide it, I will happily provide assistance.

Perfectionist

I am a perfectionist. I am unsure if this is considered a negative or positive trait. Either way, for me I try to make everything I attempt my version of perfect. If I am doing an assignment or task, I will not finish or stop doing it until its pefect. I will redo it over and over again if it is not how I want it to be.

Rafiki

“The past can hurt, but the way I see it you can either run from it, or learn from it.” – Rafiki, The Lion King.

Rafiki is a tall, long-limbed, and skinny male mandrill. He served as the Royal Mjuzi of the Pride Lands. He is eccentric and high-enegry. He has a light-hearted personality. He tends to laugh in serious serious situation but he is always there to lend a helping hand.

Even since I was a child I loved rafiki. When I was just a child, he was just a funny monkey. However, as I grew up and watched his actions and carefully listen to his words, I realized that many things that he talks about are valuable life lessons. In addition to his wise words, Rafiki is an amazing character. He is always there when the characters in the movie are about to give up and need a guiding hand.
